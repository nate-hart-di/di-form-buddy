In some instances there may be multiple types of Credit Application forms: a) DRS Short credit application forms (pre-qualify); b) DRS Full Credit Application forms; c) Other “Non-DRS “Credit Application Forms.  DRS Credit Application forms are integrated via iFrame or other similar technology.
